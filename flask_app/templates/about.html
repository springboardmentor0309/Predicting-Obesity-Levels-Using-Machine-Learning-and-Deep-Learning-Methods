About Obesity Predictor

Obesity is a complex health issue resulting from a combination of various factors, including genetics, environment, and lifestyle choices. It is defined as an abnormal or excessive fat accumulation that presents a risk to health. This condition can lead to serious health problems, including heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers.

Our Obesity Predictor uses advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze various health parameters and provide personalized predictions. By inputting your details, our model leverages patterns in the data to estimate your likelihood of obesity, empowering you to take control of your health.
